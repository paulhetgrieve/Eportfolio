CV/Resume

Professional Summary

I am a terrestrial biologist with over five years of experience in the resource conservation sector, working across invasive plant management, terrestrial and aquatic restoration, aquatic research, and long-term small mammal monitoring programs. I hold a master’s degree and have extensive experience working with diverse spatial datasets, with strong proficiency in Python and R for data analysis and ecological modelling.

Since 2020, I have led native seed collection and high-elevation restoration initiatives, implemented integrated pest management strategies, and supported multi-taxa ecological integrity monitoring programs. I thrive in both field-based and office environments and enjoy collaborative work environments where constructive feedback and continuous learning are valued.
